Generate token: (POST)
    URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/token/
    Header: {Content-Type: application/json}
    sample Json: {"username":"darshan.p", "password":"Inventory@123$"}

Manage stock: (POST)
    URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/products/1/adjust_stock/

    Header: {"Authorization": "Bearer"+ token,
             "Content-Type": "application/json"}

    sample Json: {"quantity_change": 10, "change_type": "add", "reason": "New stock received"}
